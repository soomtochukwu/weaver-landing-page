Weaver: On-Chain Reputation for Starknet

Welcome to Weaver

Weaver is the first comprehensive reputation scoring system native to the Starknet ecosystem. We help projects maximize user engagement, retention, and revenue with on-chain reputation.

What is Weaver?

Weaver introduces a pioneering reputation score system for Starknet users and projects. We combat Sybil attacks and reward genuine engagement to build trust and collaboration across the network.

Key Milestones

  • Q1 2025: Complete core scoring algorithm, smart contract architecture, MVP launch.
  • Q2 2025: Beta testing, security audits, community campaign (10k early users), mainnet launch.

Why It Matters

  • Boosts user engagement and retention for Starknet projects.
  • Strengthens ecosystem security against Sybil attacks.
  • Encourages on-chain activity and fair reward distribution.

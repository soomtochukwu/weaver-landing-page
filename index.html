Weaver: Establish Your Reputation on Starknet

Weaver is the pioneering on-chain reputation system for Starknet, engineered to foster genuine engagement, secure user interactions, and drive ecosystem growth.

Project Overview

Weaver delivers a comprehensive reputation scoring system designed specifically for Starknet users and projects. By combating Sybil attacks and rewarding verified, meaningful engagement, Weaver empowers projects to sustain and expand their user base while enhancing monetization.

Why Weaver?

Unified Reputation System

Create a standardized method to measure and reward user engagement across the entire Starknet ecosystem.

Sybil Resistance

Protect your project from fraudulent behaviors by verifying on-chain activities to ensure genuine participation.

Cross-Project Integration

Enhance network effects by allowing reputation scores to carry value and drive collaboration across multiple projects.

Innovative Engagement

Leverage task-based reputation building and transparent on-chain passports to encourage continuous user interaction.
